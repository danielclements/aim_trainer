About the game:

The aim of the game is simple, progress through the difficulties levels.
As you progress your aim and reaction time will improve.

-Start on easy difficulty, achieve a score of 30/30 then progress to the next difficulty setting.

-Jumping to pro will be challenging at first, start inproving your score and strive to achieve 30/30 hits. as you complete the pro setting progress to the extreme difficulty

-Extreme difficulty is almost impossible, achieve the highest score you can, battle friends to find out who is the ultimate aim champion!
